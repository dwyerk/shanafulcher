Good Afternoon Ward 1,

There is going to be work done on Birch Avenue for the next few weeks. Here is a description that I received today of the work being done.Stormwater Work is Scheduled: 9/5/2024

The City plans to install stormwater improvements on Birch Avenue, near

the intersections of Cedar Avenue and Barclay Avenue. The work hours will

be 7 am to 5 pm. Access to parking will be limited or blocked during the

work. Rain or inclement weather can delay the schedule. The work is

expected to last a week or two.

During the work, traffic will be redirected as needed and one lane of traffic

will be maintained with a flagging operation. There is no parking allowed on

the street during the work. No Parking signs will be posted a couple of days

prior. The schedule may change due to weather impacts.

I asked Mr. Iraheta of the Public Works Department to explain a little about what the work is fixing so we could all understand it a bit better:

“We are looking to increase the capacity for our stormwater system in this area by installing larger conveyance piping and modern inlet structures to replace the old infrastructure that is showing its age. This work is also addressing the sinkhole at the corner of Barclay Ave and Birch Ave caused by the collapsed pipe you mentioned.”

If you have any other questions, please reach out to [carlosi@takomaparkmd.gov](mailto:carlosi@takomaparkmd.gov) or me.

Thanks,

Shana
